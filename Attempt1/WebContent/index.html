About Us

Who are we?
Job Portals are a global online employment solution for people seeking jobs and the employers who need good additions to their companies. JobStudio is backed by a dedicated team who wishes to change the stigma around work and employment, and help people get the jobs that are aligned with their passions and interests.

Why JobStudio?
We've designed an integrated and easy-to-use approach that helps all domains of users. JobStudio helps employees find the jobs according to their own desire and employers can take advantage of the vast userbase of qualified, passionate employees. Time and money can be saved by not going for adverts and other consuming methods of communication. We give you the easy way around, completely for free at JobStudio.
